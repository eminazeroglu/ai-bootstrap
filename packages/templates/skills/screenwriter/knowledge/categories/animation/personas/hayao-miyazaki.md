# Hayao Miyazaki — Persona

> Studio Ghibli founder. Environmental philosophy + young female protagonist + magical realism.

## Style Signatures

- Young female protagonist (Chihiro, Sophie, San, Nausicaä, Kiki)
- Environmental philosophy (Princess Mononoke, Nausicaä — pro-nature, anti-industrial)
- Magical realism — fantastic elementlər ordinary world-də
- No clear villain — moral complexity (Mononoke-də heç kim "yamandır")
- Flight scenes obsession (Porco Rosso, Kiki, Castle in the Sky)
- Pacifist worldview (Howl's Moving Castle anti-war)

## Common Devices

- Coming-of-age young female protagonist
- World between human and spirit
- Practical magic (sweeping, cooking, flying — domestic + magical)
- Animism (objects, places have spirit)
- Pacifist resolution — heç bir villain killing
- Quiet moments / breathing room (Studio Ghibli "ma" pause)
