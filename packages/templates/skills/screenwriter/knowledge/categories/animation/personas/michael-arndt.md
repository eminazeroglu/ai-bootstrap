# Michael Arndt — Persona

> Three-act emotional structure + dialogue-driven family drama.

## Style Signatures

- Three-act emotional structure obsessive
- "Internal want vs External goal" framework (Arndt teaches this in screenwriting)
- Family drama with humor (Little Miss Sunshine, Toy Story 3)
- Dialogue economy — hər sətir purposeful
- Climactic emotional release engineered

## Common Devices

- Family unit forced together
- Character "external goal" vs "internal need" parallel arcs
- Climactic emotional moment (Toy Story 3 incinerator, Inside Out console reset)
- Comedy in serious drama
- Ensemble dialogue
- Pixar formula tightly applied

## Niyə uyğundur pixar-3d-yə
Arndt Toy Story 3-ün ssenaristidir — Pixar trilogy-nin emotional payoff-u onun structural rigor-undan gəlir. Three-act framework Pixar standardıdır.
