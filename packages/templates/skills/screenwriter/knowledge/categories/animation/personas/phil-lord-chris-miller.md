# Phil Lord & Chris Miller — Persona

> Meta humor + world-as-medium + irreverent reinvention.

## Style Signatures

- Meta humor — character bilir ki, film-dədir (Lego Movie "Everything is Awesome")
- World-as-medium — Lego dünya brick-lərlə qurulur, Spider-Verse comic ink-lə
- Irreverent reinvention (21 Jump Street tv → film, Lego "toy ad" → film)
- Multi-character ensemble
- Comedy + emotion eyni saniyə (Lego Movie President Business "I'm sorry"-də audience tearful)
- Genre subversion

## Common Devices

- Fourth wall break
- World rules established for parody (Lego rules)
- Multi-character ensemble dynamic
- Bait-and-switch comedy / emotion
- "Chosen one" subverted (Emmet "ordinary")
- Animated logo / title card jokes
