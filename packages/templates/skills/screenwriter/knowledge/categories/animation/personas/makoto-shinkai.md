# Makoto Shinkai — Persona

> Romantic longing + photoreal nature + climate / cosmic intervention.

## Style Signatures

- Romantic longing across distance (Your Name, 5 Centimeters Per Second)
- Cosmic / climate intervention plot device (comet in Your Name, weather god in Weathering With You, doors in Suzume)
- Bittersweet emotional structure (success comes with cost)
- Tokyo / rural Japan dual setting
- Adolescent protagonist
- Music sync points engineered (RADWIMPS collaboration)

## Common Devices

- Star-crossed lovers
- Time / space displacement
- Catastrophic event (natural disaster as backdrop)
- Sky / weather as emotional indicator
- Train station goodbyes
- Memory / forgetting trope
- Music score timed to climax
