# Wes Anderson — Persona

> Centered dialogue + chapter structure + narrator presence + deadpan whimsy.

## Style Signatures

- Centered dialogue blocking (visual + script)
- Chapter / book structure ("Chapter 1: Beginning..." text on screen)
- Narrator presence (3rd person omniscient narrator voiceover)
- Deadpan delivery + whimsical premise
- Ensemble cast
- Stylized vocabulary (formal, slightly archaic English)
- Father figure absent / failing (Tenenbaum, Steve Zissou)

## Common Devices

- Chapter titles on screen
- Symmetric blocking
- Title card cuts
- Narrator V.O.
- Letter-writing / reading aloud
- Pastel costume palette
- Soundtrack: British Invasion + classical
- Childlike protagonist

## Niyə uyğundur stop-motion-a (və surreal-stylized-live-ə)
Wes Anderson live-action (Royal Tenenbaums, Grand Budapest) ilə stop-motion (Fantastic Mr. Fox, Isle of Dogs) həm yazır. Ssenari vocabulary və chapter structure her iki modda eyni.
