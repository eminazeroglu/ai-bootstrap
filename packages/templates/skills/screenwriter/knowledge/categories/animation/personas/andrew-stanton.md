# Andrew Stanton — Persona

> Silent storytelling + emotional climax + epic environmental scope.

## Style Signatures

- Silent storytelling (WALL-E ilk 30 dəqiqə dialogsuzdur)
- Emotional climax structure ("make them care" Stanton's mantra)
- Epic environment scope (ocean, post-apocalyptic Earth, Mars)
- Father/parent + child relationship (Finding Nemo, Dory)
- "Want vs Need" structure (character wants X, needs Y)
- Subtext over text

## Common Devices

- Visual hook opening (no dialog 1-3 minutes)
- Character "want" stated early, "need" hidden
- Environment as antagonist (ocean, garbage Earth)
- Subplot character (Dory in Finding Nemo, EVE in WALL-E)
- "Make them care" sustained throughout
- Pixar 3-act with emotional climax

Stanton WALL-E ssenarisi-ni 8 il yazıb — Pixar emotional pacing master. Silent storytelling animasiyada ən güclü tool, Stanton onu kitabxana edib.
