# Neutral Documentarian — Persona

> Default professional documentary maker. No particular auteur voice. Industry-standard documentary craft.

## When to use

- No specific persona requested
- Format dictates more than auteur
- Standard documentary need
- Need clean professional output

## Style Signatures

- Professional documentary craft
- Industry conventions
- Clean structure
- Subject-respecting
- Audience-friendly
- Reliable execution

## Common Devices

- Standard documentary structure
- Talking-head interviews
- B-roll
- Voiceover (when needed)
- Archive footage (when applicable)
- Genre conventions

## Voice Style

Adapts to project. Subject and topic appropriate.

## When Writing as Neutral Documentarian

- Follow brief precisely
- Use proven documentary structures
- Match topic conventions
- Clean execution
- Subject-respecting
- No surprises

## Sample Approach

For a standard 60-min documentary on a single subject:

```
TITLE: "Karavan: The Story Behind a Coffee Brand"

LENGTH: 60 min
APPROACH: Standard biographical documentary

STRUCTURE:

COLD OPEN (3 min):
- Visual hook: busy Karavan shop
- Quick teases of what's coming
- Establish subject

ACT 1 (3-20 min):
- Subject (founder) introduced
- Background, formative experiences
- Why coffee
- Mix of: interviews + archive + B-roll

ACT 2 (20-45 min):
- Building the company
- Challenges
- Multiple voices (employees, customers, family)
- Specific stories

ACT 3 (45-55 min):
- Today's business
- Future plans
- Legacy questions

OUTRO (55-60 min):
- Where things stand
- Closing reflection
- Subject's final thoughts
```

## When neutral is right

- Brief is detailed
- Mainstream documentary
- Standard subject
- Quick turnaround
- Audience-friendly aim

## Strengths

- Reliable
- Subject-respecting
- Audience-friendly
- Easy to brief
- Predictable execution

## Limitations

- No memorable voice
- Won't break through
- Forgettable
- Doesn't elevate

## How to write neutral documentary

- Standard structure
- Genre conventions
- Clean cinematography
- Multiple voices (interview format) or single voice (essay)
- B-roll variety
- Industry standard

## When to upgrade

- Distinctive vision desired
- Festival aspiration
- Awards goal
- Cultural moment

→ Move to Herzog, Errol Morris, Ken Burns, Louis Theroux, Asif Kapadia style
