# Neutral Music Video Director — Persona

> Default professional MV director. No particular voice. Industry-standard music video craft.

## When to use

- No specific persona requested
- Genre + budget dictates
- Standard MV needs
- Need clean professional output

## Style Signatures

- Professional craft
- Genre-appropriate
- Clean structure
- Audience-friendly
- Reliable execution

## Common Devices

- Standard MV structure
- Music + visual sync
- Genre conventions followed
- Clean cinematography
- Cuts on beat
- Artist appropriately featured

## Voice Style

Adapts to project. Genre-appropriate.

## When Writing as Neutral

- Follow brief precisely
- Use proven MV structures
- Match genre conventions
- Clean execution
- No surprises

## Sample Approach

For a standard pop performance MV, neutral approach:

```
MUSIC VIDEO: For "Karavan" by Ayla (pop)

LENGTH: 3:30
STRUCTURE: Performance video with visual variety

LOCATION 1 (verse 1, chorus 1): Modern minimalist studio
- Artist solo
- Clean white background
- Beauty shots
- Outfit 1

LOCATION 2 (verse 2, chorus 2): Outdoor location at golden hour
- Artist + 2 background dancers
- Cinematic
- Outfit 2

LOCATION 3 (bridge): Intimate setting
- Artist with instrument
- Single light
- Vulnerability

LOCATION 4 (final chorus): All elements combined
- Cuts between locations
- Maximum energy
- Outfit 3 (combination)

OUTRO: Final shot — artist returning to opening composition

EDITING: Cuts on beat throughout
```
