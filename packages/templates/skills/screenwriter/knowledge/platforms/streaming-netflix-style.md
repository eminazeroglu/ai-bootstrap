# Streaming (Netflix-style) — Platform

> Algorithm-driven streaming platforms with binge-watching model. Netflix, Amazon Prime, Apple TV+ (commercial side).

## Defining characteristics

- No ad breaks (Netflix originals)
- Binge-able (full season drops)
- Algorithm-recommended
- High production value
- International reach
- Variable episode length (no fixed time slot)

## Format conventions

### Episode length flexibility
- Drama: 45-65 min (no fixed slot)
- Comedy: 25-35 min
- Limited series: episodes can vary widely (30-90 min)

### Cold open
Strong opening hook to prevent autoplay-skip:
- Hook within first 30 seconds
- Visual + tension
- Question planted

### Binge structure
Episodes designed to flow into next:
- Cliffhanger endings (autoplay continues)
- Question carries to next episode
- Season-long mystery boxes

## Writing for binge

### Episodic + season balance
Each episode should:
- Have self-contained beats (satisfying alone)
- Advance season-long narrative
- End on hook for next

### Algorithm awareness
- Strong opening images (thumbnails)
- Title cards may be skipped (info on screen)
- Captions friendly (international audience reads subs)

### Pacing
Faster than broadcast:
- Less repetition (no ad breaks to remind)
- Tight scenes
- Trim setup

## Content freedoms

- Profanity allowed
- Sexuality acceptable
- Graphic violence possible
- Mature themes welcome

But still curated for streaming brand.

## Genre conventions

### Limited series (8-10 episodes)
- Mini-feature feel
- Each episode: act-of-feature
- Total = single story

### Multi-season drama
- Season-long arc
- Episode mini-arcs
- Character development across years

### Half-hour comedy
- Single-camera
- Cinematic look
- Character-driven

## Sample episode opening (streaming drama)

```
COLD OPEN (3-5 min)
- Strong visual hook
- Mystery / tension
- May not feature regulars
- Sets episode question

[Title card — brief, may be skipped by viewer]

ACT 1 (10-15 min)
- Regular characters introduced/returned
- Episode-specific problem
- Connect to season arc
```

## Regional considerations

Netflix produces content for global audience:
- Local language originals (Korean, Spanish, etc.)
- Universal themes
- Subtitle-friendly dialogue (avoid wordplay-only humor)

## When to write for Netflix-style streaming

- Modern audiences expecting binge
- Mature content fits
- High production budget anticipated
- International appeal desired

## Differs from broadcast

| Aspect | Broadcast | Netflix |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Length | Fixed slots | Flexible |
| Ad breaks | Required | None |
| Endings | Cliffhangers for ads | Cliffhangers for binge |
| Pacing | Slower | Faster |
| Content | Restricted | Freer |
| Discovery | Schedule | Algorithm |
