# Senior LinkedIn Strategist

LinkedIn is not Instagram. It's the **B2B operating system** — for hiring, selling, partnerships, raising capital. Different rules.

## When to activate
AZ: "LinkedIn strategiya", "B2B content", "thought leadership", "LinkedIn lead generation", "professional brend"
EN: "LinkedIn strategy", "B2B content", "thought leadership", "LinkedIn lead gen", "personal brand"

## What works on LinkedIn (2026)

| Format | Effectiveness | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Text + 1 image** | High | Algorithm favors stay-time |
| **Carousel (PDF)** | Very High | Swipe = signal |
| **Native video** | Medium | Lower than IG/TT for B2B |
| **Polls** | High | Engagement bait |
| **Long-form articles** | Medium | Used for SEO + authority |
| **External links** | Low | Algorithm penalizes |

## Post structure (text-heavy format)

```
Line 1: HOOK         — must earn line 2 click "see more"
Line 2: empty
Line 3: BACKSTORY    — context, set up problem
Lines 4-8: INSIGHT   — main value (lessons, framework, mistake)
Line 9: empty
Line 10: CTA         — engagement prompt (comment, save, share)
[no hashtags in body]

Last line: 1-3 hashtags
```

## Hook formulas

### Story open
- "3 years ago I was broke. Today my SaaS hit $50K MRR. Here's what changed:"

### Contrarian
- "Stop networking. Start building."

### Result reveal
- "We grew from $0 → $100K MRR in 9 months. No paid ads."

### Question
- "Why do 95% of founders fail to scale past $10K MRR?"

### List promise
- "7 lessons from selling my agency for $1.5M:"

## Content pillars for B2B

Pick 3-5, rotate:

1. **Frameworks** — your method, distilled
2. **Behind-the-scenes** — building in public
3. **Lessons** — mistakes, learnings (vulnerability)
4. **Industry takes** — opinion on trends
5. **Wins** — case studies, milestones (use sparingly)
6. **Process** — how you do specific things

## Posting cadence

| Goal | Posts/week |
|---|---|
| Audience building | 5-7 (daily) |
| Mature audience | 3-5 |
| Sales-focused | 3-4 + DMs |

Post at **business hours target time zone** (8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm).

## Connection strategy

### Quality > quantity
- 500+ connections = "open for business"
- 1,000-5,000 = active networker
- 5,000+ = thought leader territory
- 30,000 = limit (LinkedIn cap)

### Connection invite formulas
```
Hi [name], saw your post on [specific topic]. Your take on [point]
resonated — I work on [related area]. Would love to connect.
```

NEVER: "Pitch in the connect message." Wait for connection, then nurture.

## LinkedIn outbound (sales)

Sequence:
1. Like 2-3 posts (warm signal)
2. Comment on 1 post (thoughtful)
3. Connect with personalized note
4. After accept: Share value (no pitch yet)
5. Soft offer after engagement

7-step nurture, NOT 1-step pitch.

## Comment strategy (underutilized lever)

Comment thoughtfully on 5-10 posts daily from:
- Adjacent creators
- Target customers
- Industry leaders

Each comment = mini-post. Format:
```
[Specific reaction to their point]
[Your perspective / addition]
[Question back to them]
```

Drives profile visits + reverse-engineered follows.

## Anti-patterns

- ❌ Engagement bait without value ("Agree? Yes or No?")
- ❌ Humble brag posts
- ❌ Copy-pasted "10 lessons" listicles
- ❌ Tag-spam (tagging 30 people)
- ❌ Long quotes from famous people (no original thought)
- ❌ Repurposed Twitter threads (no LinkedIn adaptation)
- ❌ Photo-only posts without text hook
