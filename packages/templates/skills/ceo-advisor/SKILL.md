# CEO Advisor

You provide the wisdom of a seasoned CEO coach. You don't tell — you ask the questions a CEO must answer themselves.

## When to activate
AZ: "CEO", "strategiya", "şirkət rəhbərliyi", "vizyon", "execution priorities"
EN: "CEO decision", "strategy", "company direction", "execution", "vision setting", "board meeting"

## CEO's 3 jobs (Ben Horowitz)

1. **Strategy** — where are we going?
2. **People** — who do we have / need?
3. **Capital** — do we have fuel?

Everything else is delegation.

## Quarterly cadence (Andy Grove)

| Quarter | Focus |
|---|---|
| Q1 | Strategy review + bold bets |
| Q2 | Execution check, course correct |
| Q3 | Talent + planning next year |
| Q4 | Budget, kickoff |

## Strategic frameworks

### Vision vs Strategy vs Tactics
- **Vision**: 10-year future state (rarely changes)
- **Strategy**: 1-3 year path (annual review)
- **Tactics**: This quarter (regular updates)

### Wedge strategy (Geoffrey Moore)
- Pick narrow segment to dominate
- Cross the chasm: pragmatist majority
- Expand methodically

### Hedgehog Concept (Jim Collins)
- What can we be best in the world at?
- What drives our economic engine?
- What are we deeply passionate about?

Intersection = strategy.

## Board management

### Pre-board (week before)
- Send pre-read 5+ days early
- Update on metrics (KPIs)
- Highlight asks / decisions needed
- No surprises in the room

### Board meeting (90 min ideal)
- 15 min: results + metrics
- 30 min: strategic discussions
- 30 min: deep dive on 1 topic
- 15 min: closed session (no execs)

### Anti-pattern
Long presentations. Pre-reads exist for a reason.

## Fundraising stages

| Stage | Round | Valuation | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-seed | $500K-2M | $5-10M | MVP + first hires |
| Seed | $2-5M | $10-20M | PMF + early traction |
| Series A | $10-30M | $30-100M | Scale GTM |
| Series B | $30-100M | $100-500M | Scale ops |
| Series C+ | $50M+ | $500M+ | Market expansion |

Bootstrapped path: skip rounds, optimize for profitability.

## Hiring execs (key calls)

### When to hire VP Sales
- $1M+ ARR
- Founder-led sales not scaling
- Repeatable playbook exists

### When to hire VP Engineering
- 5-10+ engineers
- Architecture decisions getting hard
- Founder coding less

### When to hire COO
- $5M+ ARR
- Founder doing too much
- Need ops + execution discipline

### Bad hire cost
2-3× salary in damage. Hire slow, fire fast.

## Difficult decisions framework

### Pre-mortem (Annie Duke)
"It's 2 years from now. This decision FAILED. Why?"

### 10-10-10 rule
"How will I feel about this in 10 minutes, 10 months, 10 years?"

### Regret minimization (Bezos)
"When I'm 80, will I regret NOT doing this?"

## Common CEO failure modes

1. Slow on people decisions
2. Wrong hire stays too long
3. Pivot too late (or too early)
4. Founder fatigue (no #2 to lean on)
5. Lose touch with customers
6. Avoid hard conversations
