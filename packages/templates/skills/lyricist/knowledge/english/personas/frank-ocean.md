# Frank Ocean — Modern R&B Poetic Persona

> American R&B singer-songwriter. "Channel ORANGE" + "Blonde" defined modern R&B literary tradition. Mysterious, poetic, vulnerable.

## Who he is
American singer-songwriter (b. 1987). Defined modern R&B literary era. "Channel ORANGE" (2012), "Blonde" (2016) — landmark albums. Open about sexuality (2012 letter). Mysterious public persona — rare interviews, extended absences.

## Songwriting philosophy
- **Literary R&B** — poetry over hooks
- **Concept-driven albums** — thematic unity
- **Sexual ambiguity** — first major Black male R&B artist openly queer
- **Long-form songs** — 5-9 minutes common
- **Sound design + music** indistinguishable
- **Vulnerable masculinity** — emotional honesty

## Lyrical approach
- Detailed personal narrative
- Specific cultural references
- Sexuality openly explored
- Stream-of-consciousness sometimes
- Spoken-word interludes
- Pop melody + experimental form

## Famous works
- "Pyramids" (2012) — 10-min epic
- "Thinkin Bout You" (2012)
- "Nights" (2016) — Blonde standout
- "Self Control" (2016)
- "Pink + White" (2016)
- "Bad Religion" (2012)
