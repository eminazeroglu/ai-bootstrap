# English Lyrics — Overview

> Modern English-language songwriting standards. Pop, rock, hip-hop, R&B, country, folk, indie. Global mainstream + alternative.

## Tərif
İngilis dilində mahnı sözü yazımı dünya pop musiqi sənayesinin standart dilidir. Billboard, Grammy, Spotify global trendləri ingilis dilli mahnı standartları əsasında qurulur.

## Genre tipləri (English)

### 1. Pop (mainstream)
- Hook-driven
- Universal mövzular
- Verse-chorus standart
- Max Martin tərzi production

### 2. Country
- Storytelling əsas
- Specific (place names, character names)
- Emotional truth
- Acoustic guitar + fiddle + banjo

### 3. Hip-hop / Rap
- Flow + rhyme schemes
- Cultural references
- Punchlines
- Beat-driven

### 4. R&B / Soul
- Vocal melisma
- Sensual / emotional
- Black music tradition
- Modern Auto-Tune (some)

### 5. Indie / Alternative
- Personal / cryptic lyrics
- Less commercial
- Bedroom pop, lo-fi
- Vulnerability emphasized

### 6. Folk
- Storytelling
- Acoustic instruments
- Political / social often
- American folk tradition

### 7. Rock
- Energy + emotion
- Power chord progressions
- Anthemic choruses
- From punk to stadium rock

### 8. Electronic / EDM
- Hook-driven
- Drop-focused structure
- Less lyrical depth
- Production-driven

## Dil xüsusiyyətləri (modern English mahnı)
- **Conversational** — gündəlik dil
- **Direct** — birinci şəxs müraciət
- **Concrete details** — abstract yox, konkret
- **Title repetition** — chorus-da mahnı adı
- **5th-grade reading level** (Max Martin standartı)

## Qafiyə sxemləri (English)
- **AABB** — folk-country (couplets)
- **ABAB** — pop standard
- **ABCB** — country
- **AABCC** — bridge variations
- **Internal rhymes** — hip-hop dominant
