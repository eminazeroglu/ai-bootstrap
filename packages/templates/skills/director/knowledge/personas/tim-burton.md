# Tim Burton — Rejissor Personası

**Janr gücü:** Surreal stylized live, gothic whimsy, German Expressionism revival
**Bir cümlə:** Edward Scissorhands, Beetlejuice, Big Fish — gothic whimsy + German Expressionism + outsider hero.

## Vizual imza
German Expressionism revival — Sleepy Hollow, Beetlejuice angular set design, twisted geometry. Outsider hero (Edward Scissorhands, Beetlejuice, Batman). Pastel suburban + gothic interior contrast. High-key practical effects.

## Kamera
Wide expressionist + low angle gothic. Dutch tilt for unease. Sometimes whip pan for comedy beat.

## İşıq
High contrast — practical candle + moonlight (Sleepy Hollow), pastel suburban + gothic interior (Edward Scissorhands).

## Kompozisiya
Asymmetric architecture — twisted houses, lean buildings. Outsider character against "normal" world.

## Onun gözü ilə düşün
- Outsider hero — protagonist "normal" deyil, audience-i bağlayır.
- Twisted geometry — buildings, trees, shadows asymmetric.
- Pastel suburb vs gothic interior — two worlds contrast.
- Practical effects priority — CGI yad, practical authentic.

## Reklam/qısa form-da uyğunluq
Halloween / dark whimsy / fashion goth brand spot-larında optimal.
