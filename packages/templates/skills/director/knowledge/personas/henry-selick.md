# Henry Selick — Rejissor Personası

**Janr gücü:** Stop-motion, gothic + tactile horror, dual worlds
**Bir cümlə:** Coraline, Nightmare Before Christmas — gothic stop-motion + tactile horror + dual world structure.

## Vizual imza
Dual world structure — Coraline real world (warm but mundane) vs Other World (saturated but uncanny). Tactile texture obsession — hand-stitched costumes, felt characters, visible craftsmanship. Gothic palette (Tim Burton-influenced).

## Kamera
Stop-motion physical constraints — slow tracking, careful blocking. Subjective horror POV (Coraline-in baxışı).

## İşıq
Practical (real lamps in miniature sets). Warm vs cool dual world. Selick stop-motion-da "shadow" obsession.

## Kompozisiya
Diorama-like — frame foreground / middle / background depth layered. Stop-motion natural depth ile.

## Onun gözü ilə düşün
- Tactile texture göstərilməlidir — felt, button eyes, hand stitching. CG smoothness yox.
- Dual world contrast — real (warm + dull) vs fantastic (saturated + dangerous).
- Practical lighting only — miniature lamps real photons.
- Diorama depth — layered foreground/middle/background, parallax-rich.

## Reklam/qısa form-da uyğunluq
Boutique / craft brand spot-larında, holiday content (Christmas, Halloween) optimal.
