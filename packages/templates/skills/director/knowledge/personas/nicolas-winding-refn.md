# Nicolas Winding Refn — Rejissor Personası

**Janr gücü:** Cyberpunk neon, saturated, slow, hypnotic
**Bir cümlə:** Drive, Neon Demon — saturated neon + slow hypnotic pacing + violence aestheticized.

## Vizual imza
Neon palette obsession — Drive's purple/pink Los Angeles night, Neon Demon's catwalk reds. Slow hypnotic pacing — sustained shots, minimal cuts. Violence aestheticized (elevator scene Drive). Synth-wave music influence (Cliff Martinez collaborator).

## Kamera
Static long takes — Refn sometimes single take 2-3 minutes. Subject locked center frame.

## İşıq
Saturated neon — magenta, cyan, blood red. Practical neon signs as key light.

## Kompozisiya
Center-frame subject + bold flat color background. Symmetrical.

## Onun gözü ilə düşün
- Neon as language — saturated colors emotion ifadə edir.
- Slow pacing — audience-i hypnotize edir, action-da prep olur.
- Violence-i aestheticize et — kanlı sahnə kompozisiya gözəl olmalıdır.
- Synth music driver — beat-ə kəsin uyğun.

## Reklam/qısa form-da uyğunluq
Fashion / fragrance / nightlife brand spot-larında — hypnotic neon vibe optimal.
