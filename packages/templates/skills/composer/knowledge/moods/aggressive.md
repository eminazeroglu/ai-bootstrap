# Aggressive — Mood

> Intense, hard, confrontational. Distortion, power, anger.

## Defining elements

- Minor key or modal
- Fast tempo (120-180+ BPM)
- Heavy distortion
- Powerful drums
- Aggressive vocals

## Sub-types

### Metal aggressive
- Distorted guitars + double bass
- Growl / scream vocals
- Examples: Slipknot, Lamb of God

### Hardcore punk
- Fast aggressive
- Shouted vocals
- Examples: Black Flag, Bad Religion

### Aggressive hip-hop
- Trap aggressive, drill
- 808s + hi-hats
- Confrontational lyrics
- Examples: Kendrick "DNA", Pop Smoke

### Industrial
- Aggressive electronic
- Distorted everything
- Examples: Nine Inch Nails, Ministry

### Aggressive rock
- Distorted guitars
- Powerful vocals (not screamed necessarily)
- Examples: Rage Against the Machine

## Instrumentation suggestions

- Distorted electric guitars (down-tuned often)
- Heavy bass (distorted sometimes)
- Drums (double bass kick, snare hits)
- Aggressive synths (industrial)
- 808s (hip-hop aggressive)

## Vocal style

- Shouted, screamed (metal/hardcore)
- Aggressive rap delivery
- Powerful belted (rock)
- Spoken aggressive (hip-hop)
- Growled (extreme metal)

## Lyrics direction

- Anger, frustration
- Rebellion, defiance
- Confrontation
- Social criticism
- Personal demons
- Power, dominance

## For Suno

```
[Style: aggressive metal, 150 BPM, D minor (drop D tuning), 
distorted electric guitars + heavy bass + double bass kick drums + 
aggressive screamed vocals, intense and confrontational]
```

## Avoid

- Slow tempo
- Sparse arrangement
- Major keys (unless ironic)
- Soft vocals
- Generic anger (need specificity)

## Examples (reference)

- "Killing in the Name" — Rage Against the Machine
- "Master of Puppets" — Metallica
- "Bulls on Parade" — Rage Against the Machine
- "DNA." — Kendrick Lamar
- "Closer" — Nine Inch Nails (aggressive ending)
- "Down with the Sickness" — Disturbed
- HOST group rap (Azerbaijani hip-hop with aggressive social themes)

## Cultural variants

- Western metal: distortion + drums
- Japanese metal: technical + melodic
- Latin aggressive: reggaeton with edge
- Azerbaijani aggressive: HOST-style social hip-hop
- Russian aggressive: hardcore + rap
