# Tense — Mood

> Anxiety, suspense, on-edge. Building tension without release (or before climax).

## Defining elements

- Often dissonant chords
- Driving rhythm or sustained drone
- Mid-fast tempo (90-130 BPM)
- Sharp dynamics shifts
- Minor or augmented harmonies

## Sub-types

### Cinematic tense (thriller)
- String tremolos
- Building drums
- Pulsing bass
- Examples: Bernard Herrmann "Psycho", Hans Zimmer thrillers

### Electronic tense
- Synth pulses
- Granular textures
- Distortion creeping in
- Examples: Trent Reznor scores

### Rock tense
- Tight rhythm
- Pre-chorus building
- Distorted guitar tension
- Examples: Radiohead "Climbing Up the Walls"

### Hip-hop tense
- 808 sub bass
- Hi-hat anxiety
- Sparse threatening
- Examples: Kendrick Lamar "DNA"

## Instrumentation suggestions

- Pulsing synth bass
- String tremolos
- Heartbeat percussion
- Distorted guitar (controlled)
- Sub-bass drone
- Sharp percussive sounds (rim shots)
- Whisper / breath sounds

## Vocal style

- Restrained but intense
- Tight, controlled
- Sometimes whisper
- Building toward release
- Male baritone or female alto

## Lyrics direction

- Internal anxiety
- Questions, paranoia
- Things unraveling
- Time pressure
- "I can't", "I won't", "what if"
- Specific sensory details (heart racing, sweat)

## For Suno

```
[Style: tense cinematic thriller, 95 BPM, D minor, sustained string 
tremolo + pulsing sub-bass + heartbeat drums + distorted synth pad, 
male baritone vocals controlled and intense, building atmosphere]
```

## Avoid

- Resolution
- Bright major keys
- Triumphant feel
- Loose loose rhythm
- Casual delivery

## Examples (reference)

- "Climbing Up the Walls" — Radiohead
- "Time" — Hans Zimmer
- "Run Boy Run" — Woodkid
- "DNA." — Kendrick Lamar
- "Black Skinhead" — Kanye West
- "Oxygène" — Jean-Michel Jarre
- "Bayatı-Kürd" — Azerbaijani mugham (tense mode)

## Cultural variants

- Western tense: orchestral + electronic
- Azerbaijani: Bayatı-Kürd mode, tar
- Middle Eastern: oud + percussion
- Modern global: hybrid orchestral-electronic
