# Ambient — Genre

> Atmospheric music. Texture over melody. Background or focused listening.

## Defining elements

- Texture-based (not melody-driven)
- No traditional song structure
- Long pieces (often 5-30+ min)
- Slow tempo or no tempo
- Sustained sounds
- Field recordings often

## Sub-types

- **Classic ambient** — Brian Eno tradition (Music for Airports)
- **Dark ambient** — eerie atmosphere (Lustmord, Atrium Carceri)
- **Drone** — sustained tones (Sunn O))), William Basinski)
- **New age ambient** — meditative (Steven Halpern)
- **Nature ambient** — environmental sounds
- **Ambient electronic** — Boards of Canada, Aphex Twin (Selected Ambient)

## Typical instrumentation

Synth pads + processed strings + field recordings + subtle percussion (or none) + occasional piano

## Production characteristics

- Heavy reverb
- Long sustained sounds
- Spatial design
- No drums often
- Layered textures

## Lyrics direction (when vocal)

- Often wordless ("aaah" / "oooh")
- Whisper-quiet if any words
- Repeated phrases
- Mantra-like

## Examples

- Brian Eno (genre founder), Aphex Twin (Selected Ambient), Boards of Canada
- William Basinski, Stars of the Lid, Tim Hecker

## Avoid

- Strong drums
- Clear song structure
- Hook-driven
- Vocal-led
- Fast tempo
