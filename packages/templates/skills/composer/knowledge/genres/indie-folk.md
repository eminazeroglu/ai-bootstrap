# Indie Folk — Genre

> Acoustic-driven indie music. Storytelling vocals, intimate production.

## Defining elements

- Acoustic guitar / piano central
- Storytelling vocals
- Mid tempo (75-110 BPM)
- Folk roots + indie sensibility
- Often introspective
- Layered harmonies

## Sub-types

- **Indie folk classic** — Sufjan Stevens, Bon Iver
- **Folk rock indie** — Mumford & Sons, The Lumineers
- **Anti-folk** — quirky alternative
- **Chamber folk** — orchestral elements
- **Modern indie folk** — Hozier, Phoebe Bridgers, Big Thief

## Typical instrumentation

Acoustic guitar + bass + soft drums + piano + occasional strings / horns + lead vocal + harmonies

## Production characteristics

- Warm, intimate
- Reverb on vocals
- Less compression than pop
- Live band feel often
- Acoustic instruments forward

## Lyrics direction

- Storytelling
- Personal, vulnerable
- Specific imagery (places, people)
- Memory, time
- Nature
- Modern life

## For Suno

```
[Style: indie folk, 90 BPM, D major with minor 7 chords, fingerpicked 
acoustic guitar + bass + soft drums + piano + occasional violin + female 
mezzo lead vocal vulnerable + male harmonies, warm intimate production]
```

## Examples

- Bon Iver, Sufjan Stevens, Phoebe Bridgers, Big Thief, Hozier
- Mumford & Sons, The Lumineers, Of Monsters and Men
- Iron & Wine, José González

## Avoid

- Heavy distortion
- Auto-tune
- EDM elements
- Aggressive vocals
- Cold modern production
