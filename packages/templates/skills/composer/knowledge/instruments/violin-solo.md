# Violin (Solo) — Instrument

> Solo violin. Most expressive of string family. Wide emotional range.

## Type
Bowed string instrument. Solo (vs section).

## Range
G3 to E7+ (over 4 octaves).

## Playing styles
- Lyrical melodies (singing-style)
- Fast passages (virtuoso)
- Pizzicato (plucked)
- Double stops (two notes)
- Folk fiddle style (rhythmic, dance)
- Klezmer (Eastern European)

## Genre fit
- Classical (concertos, sonatas)
- Folk (fiddle in country, Celtic, Eastern European)
- Indie folk (Owen Pallett, Andrew Bird)
- Film score (emotional moments)
- Jazz (rare, but exists)

## Sound character
- Singing voice quality
- Emotional, expressive
- Can be tender or aggressive
- Classical: refined
- Fiddle: rhythmic, danceable

## Sub-styles
- Classical violin
- Country fiddle
- Celtic fiddle
- Bluegrass fiddle
- Klezmer violin
- Jazz violin (rare)
