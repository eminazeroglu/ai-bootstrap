# Cello (Solo) — Instrument

> Solo cello. Deep, resonant, emotional. Often called "voice closest to human."

## Type
Bowed string instrument (low register).

## Range
C2 to A5+ (4+ octaves).

## Playing styles
- Lyrical melodies (singing line)
- Deep bass lines (fundamental)
- Pizzicato (plucked)
- Double stops
- Spiccato (bouncy bow)
- Sul ponticello (eerie)

## Sound character
- Deep, warm
- Rich overtones
- Highly emotional
- "Singing" quality
- Can be tender or powerful

## Genre fit
- Classical (Bach Suites essential)
- Film score (emotional core)
- Indie folk (warm low end)
- Pop ballads (cinematic)
- Modern composition

## Famous cellists
- Pablo Casals (classical)
- Yo-Yo Ma (modern classical)
- Hauser (popular modern)
- Cellists in pop: 2Cellos, Apocalyptica

## Mood pairings
- Melancholic, romantic, contemplative, somber, beautiful
