# Nagara — Instrument 🇦🇿

> Azerbaijani / Caucasian / Persian percussion. Drum pair. Folk celebration.

## Type
Drum pair. Different sized drums struck with sticks.

## Origin
Caucasian / Persian / Central Asian tradition.

## Construction
- Two drums (different sizes)
- Cylindrical or barrel-shaped
- Wood / metal body
- Animal skin head traditionally
- Played with sticks

## Sound character
- Punchy, declarative
- Two pitches (low + high)
- Loud, celebratory
- Ceremonial feel

## Playing styles
### Folk celebration
- Wedding music
- Cultural festivals
- Driving rhythm
- Often paired with zurna (loud reed)

### Folk dance
- Yallı dance accompaniment
- Strong rhythm

### Modern fusion
- Rock with cultural percussion
- World music

## Genre fit
- Azerbaijani folk (essential)
- Caucasian folk
- Persian folk
- World music
- Modern fusion (cultural touch)

## Cultural significance
- Wedding music (paired with zurna)
- Folk dance accompaniment
- Cultural celebration
- "Davul-zurna" tradition (drum + reed)
