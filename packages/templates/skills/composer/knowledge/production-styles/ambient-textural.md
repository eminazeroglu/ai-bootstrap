# Ambient / Textural — Production Style

> Texture-based, atmospheric. Less melody, more soundscape. Long-form.

## Defining elements

- Texture over melody
- Long sustained sounds
- Heavy reverb / delay
- Atmospheric layers
- Often slow or no tempo
- Field recordings welcome

## Sound character
- Spacious
- Atmospheric
- Meditative
- Otherworldly
- Time-suspending

## Genre fit
- Ambient (essential)
- Drone music
- Modern classical (Eno, Fennesz)
- New age
- Modern composition (some)
- Soundscape art

## Production techniques
- Synth pads (long sustain)
- Granular synthesis (modern)
- Heavy reverb (large spaces)
- Long delay (time stretching)
- Field recordings layered
- Convolution reverb (with impulse responses)
- Tape loops (Basinski tradition)
- Resampling and pitch shifting

## When to use
- Background atmosphere
- Meditation / yoga
- Film score quiet moments
- Art music
- Sleep / focus music
- Sound design (film/game)

## Famous ambient producers
- Brian Eno (genre founder)
- Aphex Twin "Selected Ambient" series
- William Basinski (decay-based)
- Stars of the Lid
- Tim Hecker
- Ólafur Arnalds (chamber + ambient)

## Length
Ambient often LONG — 5 to 30+ minutes. Not pop song format.

## Avoid pairing with
- Pop song structure
- Heavy beats
- Hook-driven approach
- Loud dynamics
- Aggressive mixing
