# Male Baritone — Vocal Style

> Standard male voice. G2 to G4 typically. Most male pop / rock / country singers.

## Range
G2 (low) to G4 (high). Comfortable middle range.

## Voice character
- Warm, full
- Conversational
- Can be intimate or powerful
- Authoritative quality

## Famous baritones
- **Country**: Johnny Cash, Chris Stapleton
- **Pop / Rock**: Sting, David Bowie, Eddie Vedder
- **Indie**: Matt Berninger (The National), Father John Misty
- **Soul**: Otis Redding, Sam Cooke
- **Crooner**: Frank Sinatra, Tony Bennett, Harry Connick Jr.

## When to use
- Storytelling songs (folk, country)
- Intimate ballads
- Most male pop (non-tenor)
- Crooner / classic standards
- Indie rock

## Lyric considerations
- Most natural male range
- Allows clear delivery
- Conversational quality

## For Suno
```
[Vocals: male baritone, warm conversational tone, intimate folk style, 
range G2-G4]
```

```
[Vocals: male baritone, storytelling country style, authoritative warmth]
```

## Default for male
If unsure, baritone is the most natural / accessible male voice for most pop / folk / country / indie / R&B.
