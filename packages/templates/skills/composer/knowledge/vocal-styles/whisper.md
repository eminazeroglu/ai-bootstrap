# Whisper — Vocal Style

> Hushed, intimate, breath-heavy delivery. Used for vulnerability / atmosphere.

## What it is
Sung very quietly with significant breath. Almost spoken. Intimate.

## Voice character
- Intimate
- Vulnerable
- Atmospheric
- Often sensual
- Close-microphone (every breath audible)

## Famous whisper singers
- **Pop**: Billie Eilish (whisper-pop pioneer)
- **Indie**: Lana Del Rey (often), Phoebe Bridgers
- **Trip-hop**: Beth Gibbons (Portishead)
- **Bossa nova**: João Gilberto (entire genre — quiet)
- **Modern R&B**: Often used
- **K-pop**: IU some softer moments

## When to use
- Vulnerable intimate moments
- Bedroom pop
- Atmospheric tracks
- Verse contrast (whisper verse → louder chorus)
- Late-night atmosphere
- Sensual ballads

## Production
- Close mic essential
- Compression to even out
- Slight reverb (intimate)
- Audible breaths kept (not removed)

## Lyric considerations
- Vulnerable themes
- Intimate confessions
- Late-night thoughts
- Often female (but male whisper exists — Bon Iver)

## Limitations
Whisper limits range and power. Best used in contrast with louder sections, or as defining aesthetic for entire track.
