# Mugham Style — Vocal Style 🇦🇿

> Azerbaijani classical vocal tradition. Highly ornamented. Microtonal. UNESCO heritage.

## What it is
Vocal style from Azerbaijani mugham classical music tradition. Master-apprentice tradition. UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage.

## Voice character
- Highly ornamented (extensive embellishment)
- Microtonal pitch bends
- Long sustained notes with vibrato
- Emotional intensity
- Improvised within mode (mugham)

## Distinctive techniques
- **Tahrir** — vocal trill / ornament (Persian-origin term)
- **Zamzama** — rapid pitch oscillation
- Microtonal pitch bends (notes between Western half-steps)
- Long melismatic phrases (many notes per syllable)
- Dramatic dynamics shifts
- Speech-like passages mixed with singing

## When used
- Mugham performance (classical Azerbaijani art music)
- Modern Azerbaijani pop with cultural touch
- Fusion (mugham + jazz, etc.)
- Cinematic Middle Eastern moments

## Cultural context
Mugham is THE Azerbaijani classical music tradition. To use mugham vocal style, ideally pair with:
- Tar + Kamancha + Daf (mugham trio)
- Azerbaijani / classical Persian language
- Modal (not chord-based) accompaniment
- Free time (rubato)

## Avoid
- Pop chord progressions
- Western 4/4 strict beat
- Auto-tune (defeats tradition)
- Pure Western delivery (no ornaments)
