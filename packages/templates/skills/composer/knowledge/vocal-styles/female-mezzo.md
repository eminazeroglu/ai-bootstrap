# Female Mezzo (Mezzo-Soprano) — Vocal Style

> Middle female voice. A3 to A5 typically. Most common female pop voice.

## Range
A3 (low) to A5 (high). Comfortable middle range.

## Voice character
- Warm, rich
- Versatile
- Can be tender or powerful
- Most relatable female voice

## Famous mezzos
- **Pop**: Adele (often sings in mezzo range), Sara Bareilles
- **Pop**: Taylor Swift, Lorde, Billie Eilish
- **Country**: Kacey Musgraves, Maren Morris
- **Soul**: Aretha Franklin (could go higher), Norah Jones
- **Classical**: Cecilia Bartoli, Joyce DiDonato
- **Indie**: Phoebe Bridgers, Lucy Dacus

## When to use
- Most pop (default female voice)
- Country
- Indie
- R&B (mid-range)
- Singer-songwriter
- Storytelling

## Lyric considerations
- Most natural female range
- Conversational quality
- Allows clear delivery
- Versatile

## For Suno
```
[Vocals: female mezzo, warm conversational pop style, range A3-A5, 
versatile and emotional]
```

```
[Vocals: female mezzo, indie folk vulnerable style, intimate delivery]
```

## Default for female
If unsure, mezzo is most natural / accessible female voice for pop / folk / country / indie. Most modern female singers fall in this range.
