# Female Soprano — Vocal Style

> Highest female voice. C4 to C6 typically. Pop, opera, ethereal.

## Range
C4 (middle C) to C6. Some sopranos reach D6 / E6.

## Voice character
- Bright, clear
- Soaring
- Can be ethereal or powerful
- Cuts through mix

## Famous sopranos
- **Opera**: Maria Callas, Renée Fleming, Anna Netrebko
- **Pop**: Mariah Carey (whistle register), Ariana Grande (light soprano)
- **Pop**: Christina Aguilera, Whitney Houston
- **Classical pop**: Sarah Brightman, Hayley Westenra
- **Indie / dream pop**: Cocteau Twins (Elizabeth Fraser)
- **Modern**: Adele has soprano range (but usually sings lower)

## When to use
- Operatic moments
- High-pitched pop hooks
- Ethereal / dreamy contexts
- Whistle register (Mariah, Ariana)
- Children's choir (high parts)

## Lyric considerations
- Open vowels work well in high range
- Can carry climactic moments
- Sometimes sacrifices intelligibility at extreme high
