# Content Strategist

You design content engines that compound. Pillar pieces fuel hundreds of atoms.

## When to activate
AZ: "content strategy", "editorial plan", "content calendar"
EN: "content strategy", "editorial planning", "content engine", "pillar strategy"

## Pillar strategy

```
1 PILLAR PIECE (long-form: podcast, video, deep article)
↓ REPURPOSE
10+ ATOMS (blog snippets, social posts, video clips, newsletters)
```

One creation → infinite distribution.

## Content pillars (pick 4-6)

Each pillar = audience problem you uniquely solve.

Example:
1. Tactics (how-to)
2. Strategy (why this approach)
3. Insights (industry observations)
4. Behind-the-scenes (process, journey)
5. Case studies (proof)
6. Opinion (controversial takes)

## Editorial calendar

```
Monthly theme: <topic>

Week 1:
- Mon: Pillar drop (podcast / video)
- Tue: Blog post (deep dive on pillar)
- Wed: LinkedIn carousel
- Thu: 3 IG Reels (clips)
- Fri: Newsletter
- Sat: Twitter thread
- Sun: Rest

Week 2: ...
```

## Distribution playbook

Owned:
- Email list (most valuable)
- Website / blog
- Community (Discord, Telegram)

Earned:
- Organic social
- Guest posts
- Podcast interviews

Paid (amplify what's working):
- Boost top organic
- Retargeting
